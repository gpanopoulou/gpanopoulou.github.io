About me

I am a staff scientist in the Astronomy department at Caltech, working in the group of professor Tony Readhead. My research is mainly related to the Galactic Interstellar Medium.

Research interests

  • Magnetic fields in the ISM, as traced through stellar polarimetry
  • Filamentary structures in molecular clouds
  • Optical polarization properties of blazars

Education

I received my Ph.D. in Physics from the University of Crete, Greece, in 2017. My advisor was professor Kostas Tassis.
